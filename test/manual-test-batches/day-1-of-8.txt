*Q1 [n1-01]* — Spot FX with a LOB to settle an import payment
WHO: Resident company (importer)
WHAT: Buying USD against Ringgit on a spot basis with a Licensed Onshore Bank to settle an import payment
AMOUNT: USD 500,000

✅ Expected verdict: PERMITTED
📌 Expected citation: Notice 1, Para 1
ℹ️ Why: Part A, Para 1: a Resident may buy/sell FCY vs Ringgit on spot basis with a LOB for its own account.

═══════════════════

*Q2 [n1-02]* — Forward FX contract with an unlicensed money changer
WHO: Resident trading company
WHAT: Entering a 3-month forward contract to sell EUR against Ringgit with a local currency dealer that is NOT a Licensed Onshore Bank, to hedge an anticipated export receivable

✅ Expected verdict: NOT_PERMITTED
📌 Expected citation: Notice 1 (no specific para asserted — judge against rationale below)
ℹ️ Why: Forward Basis transactions must be undertaken with a LOB (Part A, Para 1) — an unlicensed counterparty is not permitted for forwards.

═══════════════════

*Q3 [n1-03]* — Unregistered institutional investor using Dynamic Hedging
WHO: Resident Institutional Investor (asset manager), NOT registered under the Dynamic Hedging Framework
WHAT: Enter plain vanilla forward contracts to sell FCY against Ringgit, hedging up to 100% of its FCY-denominated securities, without documentary evidence

✅ Expected verdict: REQUIRES_APPROVAL
📌 Expected citation: Notice 1, Para 2
ℹ️ Why: Part A, Para 2 requires one-off FEP Authority registration under the Dynamic Hedging Framework before this is allowed.

*Q5 [n1-05]* — Resident unwinding a Forward Basis contract whose Firm Commitment was a Portfolio Investment
WHO: Resident asset management company

*Q8 [n1-08]* — Resident Entity transacting FX on behalf of a Group entity that is a Financial Institution
WHO: Resident Entity (treasury centre) acting for a Group entity that is itself a licensed bank
WHAT: Buying Foreign Currency against Ringgit with a LOB on behalf of a Group entity that is a Financial Institution

✅ Expected verdict: NOT_PERMITTED
📌 Expected citation: Notice 1, Para 3
ℹ️ Why: Part A, Para 3 only permits a Resident Entity to transact FX on behalf of a Group Principal that is NOT a Financial Institution or NRFI — here the Principal is a Financial Institution, so the exemption does not apply.

*Q9 [n1-09]* — Non-Resident Institutional Investor exceeding the Dynamic Hedging buy-side threshold
WHO: Non-Resident Institutional Investor, registered under the Dynamic Hedging Framework
WHAT: Buying FCY against Ringgit forward contracts amounting to 40% of its Ringgit-denominated asset exposure (which only permits up to 25% on the buy side)

✅ Expected verdict: NOT_PERMITTED OR REQUIRES_APPROVAL
📌 Expected citation: Notice 1, Para 7
ℹ️ Why: Part B, Para 7: a registered Non-Resident Institutional Investor may buy FCY against Ringgit up to only 25% of its Ringgit-asset exposure (selling is capped at 100%) — 40% exceeds the buy-side limit.

*Q11 [n2-01]* — Individual Ringgit borrowing from a Non-Resident friend, within RM1M cap
WHO: Resident individual
WHAT: Borrowing RM800,000 in Ringgit from a Non-Resident friend (not an immediate family member or employer)
AMOUNT: RM 800,000

✅ Expected verdict: PERMITTED
📌 Expected citation: Notice 2, Para 2
🚫 Must NOT mention: RM10 million, RM100 million
