2. Privacy Policy

FEP Compass is a 100% client-side application. There is no backend server. No part of this app transmits your data to any server operated by the developer, because no such server exists.

All application state — search history, activity log and in-progress form drafts — is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage, under keys such as fep_sess, fep_activity, fep_draft and fep_nav (plus small flags recording that you have completed the first-run walkthrough). This data never leaves your device — this edition of the app makes no network requests at all beyond loading its own static assets (see §3 below).

There is no analytics, telemetry, tracking pixel or third-party advertising script anywhere in this codebase. Nothing about your usage of the app is collected, logged, or reported to the developer.

There are no API keys and no accounts of any kind in this edition — there is nothing to configure and nothing to safeguard beyond ordinary control of your own device and browser profile.

This policy is written with Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA) in mind: because the app processes data entirely on-device and does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to the developer, the developer does not act as a "data user" under the PDPA in respect of your use of this app. Any personal or transaction data you type into the app remains under your own control on your own device, and (where applicable) is subject to your own organisation's data-handling policies.

3. No AI / No Third-Party Services Disclosure

This edition of FEP Compass uses no artificial intelligence and no third-party services. The Compliance Analyst's verdicts are produced by a deterministic rules engine — hand-authored boolean logic and arithmetic derived from the FEP Notice text bundled with the app — and the Reference Search runs a local BM25 keyword search over the same bundled text, entirely inside your browser.

  • No AI provider: no query, transaction detail, or chat text is ever sent to any AI model, cloud or local. The same inputs and answers always produce the same verdict.
  • No network calls: beyond loading the app's own static files (and webfonts/icons from a pinned CDN on first load), the app makes no requests. The Content Security Policy enforces this in the browser.

Where no deterministic rule covers a scenario, the app explicitly says so and shows the most relevant provisions for manual review — it does not guess.

4. Data Retention

Because there is no backend, there is no server-side data retention of any kind — the developer cannot retain data that is never transmitted to it.

Locally, data persists in your browser's localStorage until you clear it yourself, either via Settings → "Clear all local data," or by clearing your browser's site storage/cache for this app. Chat session history is automatically capped at 30 stored entries, and the activity/audit log is automatically capped at 50 entries (MAX_ACTIVITY) — older entries are dropped as new ones are added.

9. Accuracy Disclaimer
